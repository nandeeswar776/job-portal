Back-End Developer

Company: Tech Innovators Inc.

Job Description

We are looking for a skilled Back-End Developer to join our team. The ideal candidate will have strong experience in server-side logic, database management, and ensuring high performance and responsiveness of applications. You will work closely with front-end developers to integrate user-facing elements with server-side logic.

Responsibilities

  • Develop and maintain server-side logic for web applications.
  • Ensure high performance and responsiveness to requests from the front-end.
  • Integrate front-end elements developed by your colleagues into the application.
  • Optimize applications for maximum speed and scalability.
  • Implement security and data protection measures.
  • Collaborate with front-end developers, designers, and other team members.

Requirements

  • Proven experience as a Back-End Developer or similar role.
  • Proficiency in server-side languages such as Python, Ruby, Java, PHP, or Node.js.
  • Experience with databases such as MySQL, PostgreSQL, or MongoDB.
  • Familiarity with front-end technologies (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) is a plus.
  • Understanding of security and data protection best practices.
  • Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail.

Internship Details

We offer internship opportunities for aspiring Back-End Developers looking to gain real-world experience. As an intern, you will:

  • Work on live projects that impact the performance and scalability of web applications.
  • Collaborate with experienced developers to learn best practices and tools.
  • Gain hands-on experience with server-side technologies and databases.
  • Have the opportunity to transition to a full-time role upon successful completion of the internship.
