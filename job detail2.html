Job Details

Data Scientist

Company: Data Solutions Ltd.

Job Description

We are looking for a skilled Data Scientist to join our team. The successful candidate will be responsible for analyzing large datasets to uncover trends and insights that drive business decisions. You will work closely with cross-functional teams to design and implement data-driven solutions.

Responsibilities

  • Analyze complex data sets to identify patterns, correlations, and insights.
  • Develop predictive models and algorithms to support business decisions.
  • Collaborate with stakeholders to understand their data needs and translate them into actionable insights.
  • Prepare detailed reports and visualizations to communicate findings to non-technical audiences.
  • Work with engineering teams to deploy and maintain data models in production environments.

Requirements

  • Proven experience as a Data Scientist or similar role.
  • Strong proficiency in Python, R, or other programming languages used for data analysis.
  • Experience with machine learning frameworks and libraries (e.g., TensorFlow, scikit-learn).
  • Excellent problem-solving skills and ability to work with large datasets.
  • Strong communication skills and ability to present complex findings to non-technical stakeholders.

Internship Details

We offer internship opportunities for students or recent graduates who want to gain practical experience in data science. As an intern, you will:

  • Work on real-world projects that impact business outcomes.
  • Gain hands-on experience with data analysis tools and techniques.
  • Collaborate with senior data scientists and engineers.
  • Have the opportunity to transition to a full-time role upon successful completion of the internship.

Personal Details

We value a collaborative and inclusive work environment. Our office is located in the heart of New York City, offering a vibrant atmosphere and easy access to public transportation.
